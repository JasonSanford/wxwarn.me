Want to stay informed about severe weather wherever you are? We can help with that.

Requirements

To use WxWarnMe you'll need to:

  1. have a Google account
  2. opt-in to Google Latitude
  3. install/enable the Google Latitude app on your device - iOS has a dedicated app and Android has Latitude integrated with its Google Maps app.
  4. allow WxWarnMe to monitor your location (don't worry, we won't share with anyone)
Google Latitude is a location sharing service that let's you choose who (if anyone) to share your location with. Learn more about how WxWarnMe works.
