Ideas

A few ideas for premium memberships:

  • Check your location and weather alerts more often. Every 5 minutes instead of 15.
  • Since sending SMS messages can get expensive at scale ($0.01 per), maybe require premium for this.
  • Something like "My Alert Places" that let you monitor multiple locations (not just your current location) for use cases like:
    • "I live in Denver, but I want to stay informed about winter weather in the mountains during ski season."
    • "I'm a storm chaser and am interested in sever weather in various places."
    • "I'm travelling soon and want to keep an eye on the weather at x location."
    • "I have loved ones in other areas that I'd like to keep up with weather alerts for."
