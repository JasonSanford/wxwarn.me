Get Google Latitude

Latitude is an opt-in location sharing service from Google. Apps are available for both iOS and Android that automatically track your location and store it securely on Google's servers.

Sign Up for WxWarnMe

You sign up and log into WxWarnMe with your Google account. When signing up, Google will ask for your permission to allow our app to view:

  • "View basic information about your account" - Your name, timezone, etc.
  • "Manage your best-available location and location history" - We'll need access to your location to check for weather alerts near you at any given time.
  • "View your email address" - So we can notify you of alerts and stay in touch. We won't spam or sell your email address. Period.
  • "Perform these operations when I'm not using the application" - To keep you informed of severe weather, we'll need access to your location at all times, not just when your on our site.

Sit Back, Relax

We constantly track weather alerts from the National Weather Service and compare those with your current location. If we find a weather alert near you we'll notify you via email or SMS.

Stay Informed

Your email or SMS will provide valuable information about the weather alert like the severity of the event and when the alert expires. You can even view a map showing the extent of the weather alert relative to the location we tracked you.
